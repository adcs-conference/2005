Registration

Registration Form.

The registration fee is $99 (discounted to $55 for full-time students) if paid in full prior to 1st December 2005. After that date, the price will rise to $110 ($66). Both prices include GST and cover attendance at all sessions; morning tea, lunch, afternoon tea and one copy of the Symposium proceedings. To be eligible for the discount, student registrants must be studying full-time in an Australasian tertiary institution.

Registration payments can be made by credit card or cheque/money order. A registration and payment form (PDF document) is linked here. Please print the form, complete your registration and payment details, and fax/post it back.

Once we have processed your payment, a confirmation note and receipt will be emailed back to you.

At least one author of each accepted paper or poster is required to register at the time they lodge their final camera-ready copy.
